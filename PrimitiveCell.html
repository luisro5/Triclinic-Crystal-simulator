Triclinic cell (NOTE: allow pop-ups to open)

This program calculate the coordinates of the triclinic cell of the crystal, based on the parameters a,b,c as sides of the crystal and the angles (alpha, Beta, and Gamma) of the cell. The output are the coordinates of each vertex of the crystal.


parameters of the primitive cell:

side a: angle Alpha(not radians):
side b: angle Beta(not radians):
side c: angle Gamma(not radians):


cell vectors:
A=(a*sin(beta))+(b*sin(alfa)*cos(gamma))
B=b*sin(alfa)*sin(gamma)
C=(a*cos(beta))+(b*cos(alfa))+c


Coordinates x, y ,z so: 0 < n < 40
